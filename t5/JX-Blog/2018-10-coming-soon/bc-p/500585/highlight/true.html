@MarkAtTruth re: the upgrades on Fridays. This was alway the cadence we did them at Jive and we have continued that trend at Lithium to try to not "rock the boat" with extra changes. The reason we had traditionally done them on Fridays was such that we could do the upgrades, and then fix any issues that arose during the weekend before business began again on Monday. That being said, I'll be discussing with the team on the optimal day for upgrades on the next cycle and we'll weigh the pros and cons of doing them Fridays vs. Mondays.
