Cheers to 8 Years on the Lithium Community!

JennC
Retired Community Manager
‎09-06-2016 09:04 AM

The Lithium Community is celebrating our 8th anniversary this week! It’s hard to believe 8 years have flown by, and we’re taking the time this week to celebrate.

 

Celebrate with us! Throughout the week, we’ll be looking at 8 Community Lessons from our very own community thought leader @JoeC and we’ll also be recognizing 8 new communities that joined the Lithium family this year. Comment and kudo on any one of these blogs, including this one, and receive a special-edition 8th anniversary badge!

 

It’s always meaningful to take a step back and look at the past 12 months. So here are some of our milestones:

  • 9,200+ new members
  • 15 communities or community members featured in our blogs, including @JasonHill, @kkapoor, @sherrie and @ReneAvon
  • Launched the Job Board
  • 3 #OnAIrWIthLithium Hangouts: Community Resolutions, Inside B2B Communities, and most recently, Gamification Best Practices
  • Simplifying publishing through Lithium Reach
  • Added a Language Translator Integration, which allows you to view community content in French, Italian, Spanish and German—right in the community
  • A record-breaking 104 Lithy submissions, featuring your successes
  • 54 of your ideas delivered, including flexibility around setting the Accepted Solution reminder email and the ability to revoke badges.

 

What would be a community without its community members? The following members have been with us for 8 years, and we salute you:

@Gina
