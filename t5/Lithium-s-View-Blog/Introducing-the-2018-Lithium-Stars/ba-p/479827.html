Introducing the 2018 Lithium Stars!

KatherineC
Lithium Technologies
‎05-30-2018 09:43 AM

This morning, we announced the 2018 Stars slate and it was another amazing year of contribution, engagement, and talent from our awesome contributors.  We are extremely grateful for our superusers and advocates who make our community thrive and continue to push the envelope on what amazing digital customer care and engagement should look like. 

These Stars are the heart and soul of Lithium. They are thought leaders and pioneers who routinely go above and beyond to help their peers in our community, sharing best practices and  ideating with colleagues, telling their stories in our case studies, and Lithy Awards. 

It was an honor to announce the new 2018 class of Lithium Stars in our live broadcast this morning. We loved having the chance to personally recognize and thank each and every one of them. If you missed the live webcast, you can view it here:
