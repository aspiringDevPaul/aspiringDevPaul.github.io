Just a quick addition from me which I don't think has been suggested yet (although I admit I've only scan-read the other posts)...

 

I have a "retired" rank which is based on a required role I add, and I ban too, but I also have the rank set up to remove their previous roles which grant any extended permissions. You just add the names of your moderator roles into the "roles to remove box" on the rank page.

 

Might seem like overkill, but it's a handy double-check to minimise the risk of unwanted behaviour in the future, just in case the ban gets removed for any reason.

It also makes it much easier to audit your current moderators by role, as without moving their permissions, you'll eventually end up with a huge list you have to check every time you do it. Due to the previous structure of my forum (before I joined here) we had over a hundred people with moderator permissions when I joined, which was a nightmare. Through three years of attrition, that number's much higher now, and so this makes it easy to manage.

 

Thanks,
